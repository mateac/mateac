WELCOME

Welcome to the Mirakul labrador retriever kennel.

Our life with the labradors began in April 2012. when Panda (Panda Moonlight River) came from Lab's Dream kennel.She was 6- years old bitch who took our hearts. Incredible dog always in a good mood, so intelligent and warm, everything what we expected and more.Altough both of us earlier had dogs we immediately realized that labrador retriever is different.

Thanks to her in August 2013 Nikki (Lab's Dream Nala) joined us and brought a new energy. With Å½eljko Hemen's (Lab's Dream kennel) advices and support, Nikki and I starded competing and she became Croatian Junior Champion. She grew into a wonderful labrador so we decided it's time for her to have a puppies. For the father we chose Silronray's Douglas Fairbanks, a dog whose pedigree is based on the famous english bloodlines Sandylands and Tapeatom. Out of this combination we kept Ruby (Geisha) and Tosca (Gummybear).

After all of that we realized that labradors became our lifestyle so we founded kennel "Mirakul", registrated in Croatian Kennel Club and FCI, number 111/16.

Our goal is to breed healthy and nice dogs with stable character and strong will to please it's owner, but at the same time capable for work.

We would like to thank all present and future owners of our dogs for all the love and care they give to them.
